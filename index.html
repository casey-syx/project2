This highlighted the importance of usability testing to ensure that anyone visiting the website can easily find the information they need without assistance. Visual Design Enhancements

The websiteâ€™s visual design was changed completely for better consistency and appeal. Key elementsâ€”such as typography, colour, and layoutâ€”were redesigned to improve readability and reinforce branding. Similar elements are grouped together following the principle of proximity, making content easier to scan and interact with. The colour palette of brown, green, and ivory represents the natural tones of garlic while also providing high contrast for readability. By using a consistent grid layout, users can gain familiarity across different pages.

Usability Improvements

The redesigned website groups related pages for intuitive access, enhancing navigation hierarchy and making it easier for users to find information. The webpage order was also reorganized by importance, the About page now introduces Golden Acres Farm upfront, helping users quickly understand the farmâ€™s story and purpose. The Product page follows, reflecting the businessâ€™s primary focus. To make navigation more convenient, I implemented fixed navigation tabs that stay visible as users scroll through the current page, ensuring easy access to each webpage at all times.

Content Rearrangement

Information was kept the same but rearranged and simplified to deliver a more user-friendly experience. Page tabs were renamed for conciseness, and buttons are now labeled clearly with their purposes. Products are categorized by garlic types and varieties, helping users quickly find specific items. I also added a footer featuring quick access to contact information, hours, location details, and a subscription form. This ensures users can easily find essential information from any page, providing a more seamless and accessible experience.
